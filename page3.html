Mr. Hengky Antonius has been Springfield's drama teacher for 4 consecutive years. Before enterring Springfield, he has been part of multiple dance groups, performed & produced numerous musicals and plays and is currently building his social media following. Mr. Hengky has created a total of 3 musicals for Springfield. The first being Gadis dan Pohon Bernyanyi, the second being Mamma Mia and third, Sound of Music. Mr. Antonius has also started a dance group, Rebel Queens and a vocal group, Scarlett. 

With his knowledge and guidance, Springfield students are able to experience what its like to work and perform in a professional production. Mr. Antonius combines practical and theory methods of teaching to allow his students to understand and get insight on what the entertainment industry is all about. His hardwork is shown through watching the students perform on stage and improve along the way. Though he is a tough critic, he never fails to encourage his students to do their best and to never give up.
